Application Guide

Use this checklist to stay on track during the college application process.

  1. Create your student profile.
  2. Build your college list.
  3. Track deadlines for each school.
  4. Draft and revise your essays.
  5. Request recommendation letters.
  6. Submit test scores and supporting materials.
  7. Review each application before submitting.
  8. Monitor your progress in the tracker.
